# Standard Resume Template

This template mirrors Anh Dong's current resume format: a clean, 2-page, spaced-caps-heading layout with a career summary up top, core competencies block, reverse-chronological work experience with bullet-pointed accomplishments, and education at the bottom.

## Layout Structure

```
[FULL NAME — spaced uppercase]
[Phone] | [Email] | [Location] | [LinkedIn]

C A R E E R  S U M M A R Y
[2-3 sentence summary tailored to target role]

C O R E  C O M P E T E N C I E S
[Pipe-separated competency keywords — 3-4 lines, grouped by theme]
[Tool/technology categories with pipe-separated values, one line per category]

W O R K  E X P E R I E N C E

[Title]                                         [MM/YYYY — MM/YYYY or Present]
[Company], [City], [State] ([Type])
 ● [Accomplishment bullet with quantified impact]
 ● [Accomplishment bullet with quantified impact]
 ...

[Repeat for each role, reverse chronological]

Additional Experience Available Upon Request

E D U C A T I O N  &  C E R T I F I C A T I O N S
[Degree]                                        [MM/YYYY – MM/YYYY]
[School], [City], [State]

P R O F E S S I O N A L  D E V E L O P M E N T
[Course Name]                                   [MM/YYYY]
[Institution], [Format]
```

## Style Rules

- Section headings use spaced uppercase letters (e.g., "W O R K  E X P E R I E N C E")
- Job title and dates on the same line, left/right aligned
- Company line below title with city, state, and employment type in parentheses
- Bullet points use ● character with one space indent
- Each bullet starts with a past-tense action verb (present tense for current role)
- Every bullet includes a quantified metric where possible
- Tools/technologies mentioned in parentheses at end of bullet when relevant
- Target length: 2 pages
- Core competencies block groups: domain skills first, then tool categories by type
